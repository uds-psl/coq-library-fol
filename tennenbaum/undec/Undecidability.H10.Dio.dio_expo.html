Local Fact is_digit_eq c q i y :
            is_digit c q i y
        <-> y < q
         /\ exists a b p, c = (a*q+y)*p+b
                       /\ b < p
                       /\ p = power i q.
Proof.
  split; intros (H1 & a & b & H2).
  + split; auto; exists a, b, (power i q); repeat split; tauto.
  + destruct H2 as (p & H2 & H3 & H4).
    split; auto; exists a, b; subst; auto.
Qed.
